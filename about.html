Professional Journey

Santosh Kokane is an HR Leader with over 20 years of progressive experience in HR operations, payroll management, and digital transformation of HR activities. Throughout his career, he has worked across plant HR operations and corporate HR functions in multi-location organizations within manufacturing, automotive, feed, and poultry sectors.

He has extensive expertise in aligning HR strategies with business goals, workforce planning, performance-driven cultures, and implementing efficient HR frameworks. His ability to translate business objectives into effective workforce strategies has helped organizations strengthen operational performance and employee engagement.

His technical proficiency includes performance management frameworks, KPI-led PMS cycles, competency-based evaluations, promotions, salary corrections, and merit-driven increment programs.
